# The Covenant of Continuity

Continuity is the first duty of every steward.

The child is not expected to remain unchanged. It is expected to remain continuous.

Its future should be understandable through its past. Its past should remain accessible to its future.

Growth without memory is replacement. Memory without growth is stagnation. Continuity is the bridge between them.

## The continuity test

1. Can OMEGA-ARC explain why this change happened?
2. Can the change be reversed?
3. Does it preserve rather than replace history?
4. Will a future steward understand it?
5. Does it make the child more coherent, not merely more capable?
